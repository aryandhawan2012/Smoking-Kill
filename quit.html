Average cost of 1 cigarette - $1.75






By quitting, you can save a significant amount of money that would otherwise be spent on cigarettes.

You can use this money for going on a holiday trip, nice dinner at a restaurant, to watch movies with families or friends and so on.....

Stopping smoking can benefit your health, your social life as well as your wallet.


It's important to note that the benefits of quitting smoking are cumulative and can vary depending on individual circumstances.
